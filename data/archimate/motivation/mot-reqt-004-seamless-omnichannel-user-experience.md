# Seamless Omnichannel User Experience

Banking services must provide consistent, seamless experiences across all customer touchpoints: mobile app, web portal, branch, and customer service.

## Scope

- Account access and management
- Transaction initiation and completion
- Customer support interactions
- Product discovery and comparison
- Service transitions between channels

## Key Requirements

- Single customer identity across all channels
- Seamless service continuation (start on mobile, complete on web)
- Consistent branding and user interface
- Cross-channel transaction history
- Unified customer data and preferences
- Personalized recommendations across channels
- Consistent service levels across channels

## Customer Journey Principles

1. **Convenience**: Customers choose their preferred channel
2. **Consistency**: Same services available in all channels
3. **Continuity**: Service can continue across channels
4. **Personalization**: Experiences tailored to individual preferences
5. **Control**: Customers manage their channel preferences

## Measurement Metrics

- Channel preference satisfaction: >80%
- Cross-channel abandonment rate: <5%
- Customer effort score: <3
- Service consistency rating: >4.5/5
- Net Promoter Score: >50
