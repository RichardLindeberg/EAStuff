# Customer Trust

Establish the bank as a trusted, transparent, and socially responsible financial partner through ethical practices and strong data protection.

## Description

Nordic markets have strong expectations for corporate responsibility, sustainability, and transparency. This goal ensures the bank maintains the trust of customers and society by operating ethically and protecting customer interests.

## Objectives

1. **Data Protection Excellence**: Implement industry-leading data security and privacy practices
2. **Transparent Operations**: Clearly communicate fees, terms, and business practices
3. **Sustainable Investing**: Offer ESG-aligned investment options and reporting
4. **Ethical Lending**: Maintain responsible lending practices and customer support
5. **Green Banking**: Reduce environmental footprint and support renewable energy financing

## Success Metrics

- Customer trust index: >70%
- Data security incidents: Zero breaches
- Sustainability scores: Top quartile in Nordic banking
- Customer complaint resolution rate: >95%
- ESG-aligned products adoption: >30% of portfolio

## Strategic Alignment

Trust is the foundation of banking relationships in Nordic markets. This goal differentiates the bank in a competitive market and supports long-term customer retention and profitability.
