# Data-Driven Decision Making

The bank makes business decisions based on accurate data, analytics, and evidence rather than intuition or historical practices.

## Principle Statement

We collect, analyze, and act on data to inform all business decisions. Data quality, governance, and accessibility are foundational capabilities. We combine data insights with human judgment to make informed decisions.

## Implementation

- Data governance framework and standards
- Data quality and lineage management
- Advanced analytics and business intelligence
- AI and machine learning for predictive insights
- Regular performance reporting and dashboards
- Evidence-based decision-making processes
- Data literacy and training programs

## Data Capabilities

- Customer analytics and segmentation
- Financial and risk analytics
- Operational performance metrics
- Market and competitive intelligence
- Predictive models for credit and fraud
- Real-time dashboards and alerts

## Data Governance

- Data ownership and accountability
- Privacy and security by design
- Regulatory compliance (GDPR, etc.)
- Data ethics and responsible AI
- Transparent algorithms and decision-making
- Audit trails and data lineage

## Measurement

- Data quality score: >95%
- Analytics infrastructure uptime: >99.95%
- Decision-making based on data: >80% of strategic decisions
- Analytics adoption across organization: >75% of staff
- Time to insight: <24 hours for standard reports

## Stakeholder Impact

- **Customers**: Better personalized services and offers
- **Management**: Informed strategic decisions
- **Employees**: Data-driven goals and performance feedback
- **Regulators**: Transparent, evidence-based practices
- **Shareholders**: Optimized business performance
