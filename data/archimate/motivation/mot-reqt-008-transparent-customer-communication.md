# Transparent Customer Communication

All customer communications regarding fees, terms, services, and changes must be clear, timely, and easily understandable.

## Communication Scope

- Fee and pricing information
- Service terms and conditions
- Product features and limitations
- Changes to services or policies
- Account security and fraud alerts
- Complaint resolution status
- Data handling and privacy practices
- Performance reporting (investments, loans)

## Transparency Standards

- Plain language (avoid jargon, technical terms)
- Clear fee structures with no hidden charges
- Advance notice of service changes (30+ days)
- Multiple communication channels
- Accessible formats (digital, print, accessible)
- Multilingual support where applicable
- Easy escalation for issues or questions

## Digital Communication Requirements

- Clear in-app notifications for important messages
- Accessible website privacy and terms pages
- Simple fee calculators and comparison tools
- Proactive account alerts and recommendations
- Easy access to account history and documentation
- Feedback mechanisms for customer input

## Measurement Metrics

- Customer understanding of fees: >85%
- Communication clarity rating: >4/5
- Customer satisfaction with communication: >80%
- Complaint rates related to communication: <1%
- Response time to customer inquiries: <24 hours
