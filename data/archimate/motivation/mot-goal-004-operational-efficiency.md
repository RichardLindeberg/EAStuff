# Operational Efficiency

Reduce operational costs and improve productivity through process automation, cloud migration, and modern technology infrastructure.

## Description

Nordic banking is facing margin compression due to low interest rates and regulatory costs. This goal focuses on achieving operational excellence through systematic process optimization and technology modernization.

## Objectives

1. **Reduce Manual Processes**: Automate 60% of routine operational tasks
2. **Migrate to Cloud**: Move infrastructure to secure cloud platforms for scalability
3. **Consolidate Systems**: Reduce technology footprint from legacy systems
4. **Optimize Staffing**: Reallocate resources from operational tasks to customer-facing roles
5. **Improve Data Quality**: Establish single source of truth for critical business data

## Success Metrics

- Operational cost reduction: 20-25% within 3 years
- Process automation coverage: >60% of operational processes
- Cloud infrastructure: >70% by 2027
- Employee productivity increase: >30%
- System outage time: <99.9% availability

## Strategic Alignment

This goal enables competitive pricing and maintains profit margins while reinvesting savings into customer experience and digital capabilities.
