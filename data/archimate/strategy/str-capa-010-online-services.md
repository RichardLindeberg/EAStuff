# Online Banking Services Capability

Strategic capability to deliver comprehensive banking services through digital channels accessible 24/7 from anywhere.

## Description

Online Banking Services represents the bank's strategic capability to provide customers with full-service banking through internet-based platforms. This capability enables Nordic bank customers to manage accounts, make payments, apply for products, and access financial services without visiting physical branches.

## Capability Components

### Account Management
- View account balances and statements
- Download transaction history
- Manage account settings and preferences
- Set up alerts and notifications

### Payment Services
- Domestic and international transfers
- Bill payments and standing orders
- Mobile payment integrations (Swish, Vipps, MobilePay)
- Instant payments (SCT Inst)

### Product Services
- Apply for cards, loans, and accounts
- Manage existing products
- View and accept product offers
- Digital signing and document handling

### Customer Service
- Secure messaging with advisors
- Video banking appointments
- Self-service knowledge base
- Chatbot assistance

## Nordic Market Context

This capability is critical in the Nordic region where:
- Over 95% of banking customers use online banking
- Digital-first expectations are extremely high
- Strong e-ID infrastructure enables secure digital services
- Regulatory requirements (PSD2, DORA) mandate robust digital services

## Maturity Level

Currently at Level 4 (Managed):
- Established processes and continuous monitoring
- Integrated with other capabilities
- Performance metrics tracked and optimized
- Regular updates and improvements

Target: Level 5 (Optimizing) by 2027
