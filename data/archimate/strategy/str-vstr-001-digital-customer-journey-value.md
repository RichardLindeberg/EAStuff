# Digital Customer Journey Value Stream

Complete value stream encompassing how customers discover, evaluate, onboard with, and engage with the bank through digital channels.

## Value Stream Description

This value stream maps the complete digital customer experience from initial awareness through long-term engagement, identifying opportunities to create customer value at each stage.

## Stages

### 1. Awareness and Discovery
- Digital marketing and advertising
- Social media engagement
- Fintech comparison websites
- App store reviews and ratings
- Referral programs
**Value Created**: Customer awareness and consideration

### 2. Evaluation and Comparison
- Website information and resources
- Mobile app trial/preview
- Comparison tools and calculators
- Customer reviews and testimonials
- Financial education content
**Value Created**: Informed decision-making

### 3. Rapid Digital Onboarding
- Mobile app account opening (15 minutes)
- Digital identity verification
- KYC/AML verification
- Instant account activation
- Welcome support and guidance
**Value Created**: Frictionless entry, time savings

### 4. Account Management and Services
- Account access and information
- P2P payments and transfers
- Bill payments
- Investment and savings products
- Loan and credit applications
**Value Created**: Convenient service access

### 5. Personalized Engagement
- Relevant product recommendations
- Personalized offers and rates
- Financial insights and advice
- Proactive support and alerts
- Community and social features
**Value Created**: Tailored financial solutions

### 6. Customer Support and Service
- 24/7 mobile support
- AI-powered chatbot assistance
- Video call with advisors
- In-app help and guides
- Social media support
**Value Created**: Always-available assistance

### 7. Ecosystem Participation
- Third-party integrations (fintech)
- Open banking connections
- Embedded finance experiences
- API-based integrations
- Partner services
**Value Created**: Extended financial ecosystem

## Customer Outcomes

- **Convenience**: Banking available anytime, anywhere
- **Speed**: Fast account opening, instant transactions
- **Personalization**: Tailored products and services
- **Transparency**: Clear fees and terms
- **Trust**: Secure, privacy-protected interactions
- **Choice**: Multiple channels and service options

## Business Outcomes

- Customer acquisition cost reduction
- Increased digital channel adoption
- Higher customer lifetime value
- Improved retention and loyalty
- Operational cost reduction
- Competitive differentiation

## Key Performance Indicators

- Digital channel adoption: >75%
- Mobile app penetration: >60% of customers
- Digital transaction volume: >70%
- Customer acquisition cost (digital): <50% of branch
- Digital customer lifetime value: >150% of branch
- Net Promoter Score: >50
