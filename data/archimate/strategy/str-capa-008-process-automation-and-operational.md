# Process Automation and Operational Excellence Capability

Strategic capability to identify, optimize, and automate operational processes to achieve efficiency, reduce costs, and improve consistency and quality.

## Description

This capability systematically identifies opportunities for automation, implements RPA and workflow automation solutions, and applies continuous improvement methodologies to optimize banking operations.

## Capability Components

### People
- Process improvement specialists
- RPA developers
- Workflow automation specialists
- Business process analysts
- Change management specialists
- Quality assurance specialists

### Process
- Process discovery and mapping
- Opportunity identification and prioritization
- Process optimization and redesign
- Automation solution development
- Change management and training
- Performance monitoring
- Continuous improvement

### Technology
- Robotic Process Automation (RPA) platform
- Workflow automation engines
- Process mining and analytics tools
- Business process management (BPM) systems
- Integration middleware
- Performance monitoring tools
- Low-code/no-code platforms

### Information
- Process maps and documentation
- Automation inventory
- Cost-benefit analysis
- Performance metrics
- Automation ROI tracking
- Process improvement roadmaps

## Current State

- Manual operational processes
- High manual labor costs
- Inconsistent process execution
- Limited automation (20% coverage)
- Maturity: 1 - Initial

## Target State

- 60% process automation coverage
- 20-25% labor cost reduction
- 50% average cycle time reduction
- >95% first-pass accuracy
- Maturity: 4 - Managed

## Strategic Value

- Operational cost reduction
- Improved process consistency
- Faster customer service
- Reduced errors and compliance risk
- Staff redeployment to value-added work
