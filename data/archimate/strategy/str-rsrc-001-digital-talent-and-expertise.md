# Digital Talent and Expertise Resources

Critical resource of skilled digital talent including software engineers, data scientists, UX designers, and other technical expertise required to execute digital transformation.

## Description

The bank requires significant digital talent to build modern banking platforms, operate cloud infrastructure, and implement AI/ML capabilities. This includes attracting, developing, and retaining top technical talent.

## Resource Components

### External Acquisition
- Recruitment of software engineers (Java, Python, Go, Node.js)
- Data scientists and ML engineers
- UX/UI designers and product designers
- Cloud architects and DevOps engineers
- API and integration specialists
- Cybersecurity specialists

### Internal Development
- Technical training programs
- Leadership development for technical staff
- Continuous learning and certifications
- Internal mobility and career development
- Mentoring and coaching programs

### Partnerships
- University partnerships and graduate recruitment
- Consultancy partnerships for specialized skills
- Nearshore and offshore development partnerships
- Training and education providers

## Current Gaps

- Limited cloud expertise
- Insufficient AI/ML talent
- Shortage of modern software engineers
- Limited product management capacity
- Talent retention challenges

## Acquisition Strategy

- Competitive compensation and benefits
- Flexible work arrangements
- Career development opportunities
- Innovation and learning culture
- Purpose-driven mission
- Diversity and inclusion initiatives

## Investment

- Recruitment budget: 20% increase over 2026-2027
- Training budget: 5% of payroll
- Nearshore partnerships: EUR 5M annually
- University partnerships and internship programs
