# Beneficial Ownership Verification Instruction


**Purpose:**
Define the steps to identify and verify beneficial owners for legal entities.

## Scope
- Corporate, SME, and institutional customers.
- New onboarding and periodic reviews.

## Prerequisites
- Corporate structure documents
## Procedure
1. Collect corporate structure and ownership declarations.
2. Identify all beneficial owners meeting thresholds.
3. Verify beneficial owner identity and documentation.
4. Screen beneficial owners for sanctions and PEP status.
5. Record ownership percentages and evidence.
6. Obtain compliance sign-off for completion.

## Outputs and Records
- Beneficial owner register
- Verification evidence and screening results
