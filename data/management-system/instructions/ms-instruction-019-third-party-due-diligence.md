# Third-Party Due Diligence Instruction


**Purpose:**
Provide steps for evaluating third-party risk before onboarding or renewal.

## Scope
- Vendors, partners, and outsourced service providers.

## Prerequisites
- Completed vendor intake form
- Proposed contract and service description

## Procedure
1. Classify vendor criticality and data access.
2. Collect security and compliance evidence.
3. Review financial stability and operational resilience.
4. Assess regulatory, legal, and data protection risks.
5. Document risk rating and required mitigations.
6. Obtain approval before contract execution.

## Outputs and Records
- Due diligence report
- Risk rating and mitigation plan
