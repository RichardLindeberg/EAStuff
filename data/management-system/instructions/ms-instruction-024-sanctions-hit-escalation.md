# Sanctions Hit Escalation Instruction (EU AMLR)

**Purpose:**
Provide steps for assessing and escalating potential sanctions hits in line with EU AMLR expectations and applicable EU sanctions regimes.

## Scope
- Screening hits from onboarding and ongoing transaction monitoring.
- All products, channels, and customer types.

## Prerequisites
- Screening alert case file
- Customer and counterparty data

## Procedure
1. Validate alert data quality and resolve obvious false positives.
2. Perform enhanced name matching and contextual analysis.
3. Escalate potential true matches to sanctions specialists.
4. Apply holds or blocks when required by sanctions obligations.
5. Document decisions, evidence, and regulatory notifications if applicable.
6. Review customer risk rating and trigger EDD if needed.

## Outputs and Records
- Sanctions alert disposition record
- Escalation evidence and decision log
