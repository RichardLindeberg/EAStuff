**Purpose:**
Define steps for assessing creditworthiness and documenting decisions.

## Scope
- Consumer and business loan applications.

## Prerequisites
- Complete loan application and documentation
- Credit bureau data and income verification

## Procedure
1. Validate application completeness and identity.
2. Retrieve credit bureau and external data.
3. Calculate affordability and debt ratios.
4. Assign risk rating and recommended terms.
5. Escalate exceptions to credit committee.
6. Record decision and rationale in the system.

## Outputs and Records
- Credit assessment report
- Decision record and approvals
