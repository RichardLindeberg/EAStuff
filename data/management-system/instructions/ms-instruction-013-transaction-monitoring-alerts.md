# Transaction Monitoring Alert Handling Instruction


**Purpose:**
Provide steps for triaging, investigating, and resolving monitoring alerts.

## Scope
- Alerts from sanctions screening and transaction monitoring systems.
- All payment channels and products.

## Prerequisites
- Alert case record
- Customer profile and risk rating
- Recent transaction history

## Procedure
1. Validate alert data and confirm customer identity.
2. Review historical activity and expected behavior.
3. Determine if escalation is required.
4. Document findings and resolution outcome.
5. Escalate to SAR workflow when warranted.

## Outputs and Records
- Alert disposition record
- Escalation notes and evidence
