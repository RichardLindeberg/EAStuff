# PEP Screening and Approval Instruction (EU AMLR)

**Purpose:**
Define steps for identifying, screening, and approving politically exposed persons (PEPs) in line with EU AMLR requirements.

## Scope
- All new and existing customers.
- Related parties: beneficial owners, controlling persons, authorized representatives.

## Prerequisites
- Customer profile and onboarding documentation
- Screening tools and updated PEP lists

## Procedure
1. Screen customers and related parties against PEP lists at onboarding.
2. Classify PEP status (domestic, foreign, international organization) and capture basis.
3. Trigger enhanced due diligence for all confirmed PEPs.
4. Obtain senior management approval before establishing or continuing the relationship.
5. Set heightened monitoring and review cadence for PEP relationships.
6. Record decisions, rationale, and evidence in the case file.

## Outputs and Records
- PEP classification record
- Approval evidence and monitoring plan
