# Fraud Case Investigation Instruction


**Purpose:**
Provide steps to investigate, document, and resolve fraud cases.

## Scope
- Payment fraud, account takeover, and application fraud.

## Prerequisites
- Fraud alert case record
- Customer profile and transaction history

## Procedure
1. Validate alert details and confirm incident scope.
2. Gather supporting evidence and logs.
3. Contact customer for verification if required.
4. Decide on blocking, recovery, and remediation actions.
5. Document findings and update detection rules.

## Outputs and Records
- Fraud investigation report
- Customer communication record
