**Purpose:**
Define required steps for SAR investigation and reporting.

## Scope
- All suspicious activity cases across products and channels.

## Prerequisites
- Case file with supporting evidence
- Compliance review notes

## Procedure
1. Confirm the threshold for SAR filing is met.
2. Compile required customer and transaction details.
3. Prepare SAR narrative and evidence attachments.
4. Obtain MLRO approval.
5. Submit SAR via the approved channel.
6. Record submission and retain evidence.

## Outputs and Records
- SAR submission record
- Investigation evidence and narrative
