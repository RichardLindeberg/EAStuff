**Purpose:**
Define steps to complete EDD for high-risk customers and transactions.

## Scope
- High-risk customers, PEPs, and elevated risk relationships.

## Prerequisites
- High-risk trigger record
- Customer risk assessment data

## Procedure
1. Confirm the EDD trigger and scope.
2. Collect additional identity and ownership evidence.
3. Validate source of wealth and source of funds.
4. Review adverse media and sanctions results.
5. Document enhanced risk rating and rationale.
6. Obtain senior management approval if required.

## Outputs and Records
- EDD assessment report
- Evidence log and approvals
