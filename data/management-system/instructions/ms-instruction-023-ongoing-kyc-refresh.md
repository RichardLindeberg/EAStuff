# Ongoing KYC Refresh Instruction (EU AMLR)

**Purpose:**
Define a consistent approach for periodic KYC refreshes and event-driven reviews required under EU AMLR.

## Scope
- Retail, SME, corporate, and institutional customers.
- Triggered reviews based on risk changes or significant events.

## Prerequisites
- Current customer profile and risk rating
- Latest identification and ownership evidence

## Procedure
1. Determine review frequency by risk tier and product exposure.
2. Perform event-driven refresh for material changes (ownership, control, activity).
3. Validate customer identity, ownership, and control data.
4. Update risk rating and document the rationale.
5. Review sanctions and PEP screening results.
6. Record completion, evidence, and any remediation actions.

## Outputs and Records
- Updated KYC profile and risk rating
- Evidence log and remediation actions
