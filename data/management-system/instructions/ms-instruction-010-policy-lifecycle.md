# Policy Lifecycle Instruction


**Purpose:**
Provide a step-by-step process for creating, approving, publishing, and reviewing governance policies.

## Scope
Applies to all new policies and material revisions to existing policies.

## Prerequisites
- Assigned policy owner and sponsor
- Draft scope and objectives
- Applicable regulatory references identified

## Procedure
1. Draft the policy using the approved template and metadata fields.
2. Map the policy to relevant enterprise capabilities and related instructions/manuals.
3. Obtain review from Risk and Compliance.
4. Obtain approval from the designated authority.
5. Publish the policy in the governance repository and notify impacted teams.
6. Schedule the next review date and assign owners for updates.

## Outputs and Records
- Approved policy document
- Review and approval record
- Publication notice
