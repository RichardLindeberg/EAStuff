# KYC Onboarding Instruction


**Purpose:**
Provide a step-by-step process for completing KYC onboarding and risk classification.

## Scope
- New retail and corporate customers.
- Digital and assisted onboarding channels.

## Prerequisites
- Valid identification documents
- Completed onboarding form
- Customer risk assessment checklist

## Procedure
1. Capture customer identity and contact details.
2. Verify identity using approved sources and tools.
3. Screen customer against sanctions and PEP lists.
4. Assign initial risk rating and document rationale.
5. Capture required consents and disclosures.
6. Confirm record completeness and archive evidence.

## Outputs and Records
- Verified customer profile
- KYC checklist and screening results
- Risk rating record
