# Governance System Manual


**Purpose:**
Provide a reference overview of the governance system, including document types, ownership, and how governance links to enterprise architecture.

## Audience
Governance owners, risk and compliance teams, and enterprise architecture practitioners.

## Overview
The governance system is the controlled set of policies, instructions, and manuals that define how work is directed, performed, and improved. Each document is versioned, approved, and linked to the enterprise capabilities it governs.

## Processes and Procedures
- Policy lifecycle management
- Exception management and approvals
- Review and update cadence
- Traceability to enterprise capabilities and requirements
