# PEP Management Manual (EU AMLR)

**Purpose:**
Provide practical guidance for identifying, approving, and monitoring PEP relationships in line with EU AMLR.

## Audience
Financial crime operations, compliance, and relationship managers.

## Key Topics
- PEP identification and classification rules.
- Senior management approval workflow.
- Enhanced monitoring and periodic review cadence.

## Operational Guidance
- Evidence standards for PEP determination.
- Required controls for source of wealth and source of funds.
- Trigger-based escalation and exit criteria.

## Records
- PEP assessment files and approvals
- Monitoring plans and review outcomes
