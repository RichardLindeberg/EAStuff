# FIU Reporting and STR Manual (EU AMLR)

**Purpose:**
Provide operational guidance for suspicious transaction reporting and FIU engagement consistent with EU AMLR.

## Audience
Financial crime operations and compliance teams.

## Overview
- STR intake, investigation, and approval workflow.
- Coordination with legal and regulatory reporting channels.

## Operational Guidance
- Evidence collection and case documentation standards.
- Narrative quality checks and submission controls.
- Record retention and confidentiality requirements.

## Records
- STR case files and submission logs
- Evidence and approval records
