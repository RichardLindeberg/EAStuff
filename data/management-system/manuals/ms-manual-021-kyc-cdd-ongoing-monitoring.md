# KYC/CDD Ongoing Monitoring Manual (EU AMLR)

**Purpose:**
Provide operational guidance for periodic KYC refreshes and ongoing monitoring under EU AMLR.

## Audience
Compliance operations, onboarding teams, and relationship managers.

## Overview
- Risk-tiered review schedules and event-driven triggers.
- Documentation standards for ownership and control updates.

## Operational Guidance
- Identify review triggers and assign case ownership.
- Refresh identity, ownership, and control evidence.
- Recalculate risk scores and adjust monitoring intensity.
- Escalate high-risk cases for EDD.

## Records
- Review schedule and completion logs
- Updated KYC evidence and risk rationale
