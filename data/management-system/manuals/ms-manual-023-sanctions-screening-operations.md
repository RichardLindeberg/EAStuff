# Sanctions Screening Operations Manual (EU AMLR)

**Purpose:**
Provide operational guidance for sanctions screening and alert handling aligned with EU AMLR expectations.

## Audience
Financial crime operations and compliance teams.

## Overview
- Screening coverage for onboarding and transactions.
- Alert disposition categories and escalation paths.

## Operational Guidance
- Data quality and name matching standards.
- Handling potential true matches and documentation requirements.
- Coordination with legal and compliance for regulatory notifications.

## Records
- Sanctions alert logs and case files
- Decision logs and audit trails
