# Data Protection and Privacy Policy


**Purpose:**
Establish requirements for lawful data processing, privacy controls, and customer rights.

## Scope
- Customer, employee, and partner personal data.
- Data collected, stored, processed, or shared by the bank.

## Policy Statement
- Personal data is processed lawfully and minimally.
- Data subject rights are fulfilled within required timelines.
- Privacy controls are built into systems and processes.

## Roles and Responsibilities
- Privacy Office: Owns policy and guidance.
- Data Owners: Ensure compliant processing.
- Security: Protects data confidentiality and integrity.

## Compliance and Exceptions
Exceptions require privacy impact assessment and approval.
