**Purpose:**
Define third-party risk oversight for vendors, partners, and outsourced services.

## Scope
- All third-party relationships, including cloud and SaaS providers.
- Material outsourcing and critical service providers.

## Policy Statement
- Due diligence is required before onboarding a third party.
- Contracts include security, resilience, and audit requirements.
- Ongoing monitoring is required for critical vendors.

## Roles and Responsibilities
- Vendor Management: Coordinates due diligence and onboarding.
- Risk: Sets risk ratings and monitoring standards.
- Technology: Validates security and resilience controls.

## Compliance and Exceptions
Exceptions require CRO approval and documented mitigation.
