# KYC/CDD Policy


**Purpose:**
Set mandatory standards for customer identification, verification, and risk classification in support of the AML/CTF policy and EU AMLR requirements.

## Scope
- Retail, corporate, and private banking customers.
- New onboarding, periodic reviews, and event-driven refreshes.

## Policy Statement
- Identity verification is required before account activation.
- Risk ratings drive monitoring intensity and review cadence.
- Beneficial ownership must be identified for legal entities.
- PEP identification and EDD are mandatory for higher-risk relationships.

## Roles and Responsibilities
- Front Office: Collects required documentation.
- Compliance: Sets standards and approves exceptions.
- Operations: Ensures records are complete and retained.

## Compliance and Exceptions
All exceptions require compliance approval and documented mitigations.
