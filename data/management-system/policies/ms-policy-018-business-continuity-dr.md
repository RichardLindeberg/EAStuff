**Purpose:**
Ensure critical services continue through disruptions and recover within defined targets.

## Scope
- All critical business processes and technology services.
- In-house and outsourced services.

## Policy Statement
- Recovery objectives are defined for critical services.
- Continuity plans are tested annually.
- Third-party recovery capabilities are validated.

## Roles and Responsibilities
- Business Continuity: Owns plans and exercises.
- Technology: Maintains recovery infrastructure.
- Business Units: Maintain process-level playbooks.

## Compliance and Exceptions
Exceptions require executive approval and remediation plans.
