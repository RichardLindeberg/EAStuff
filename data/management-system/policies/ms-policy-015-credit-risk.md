# Credit Risk Policy


**Purpose:**
Set credit risk appetite, underwriting standards, and portfolio monitoring expectations.

## Scope
- Consumer and business lending products.
- New originations and renewals.

## Policy Statement
- Lending decisions follow documented credit criteria.
- Exceptions require formal approval and tracking.
- Portfolio risk is monitored against limits.

## Roles and Responsibilities
- Credit Risk: Defines policies and limits.
- Lending: Executes underwriting and approvals.
- Finance: Monitors portfolio performance.

## Compliance and Exceptions
All exceptions must be recorded and reviewed.
