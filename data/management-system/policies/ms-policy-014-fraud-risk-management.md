# Fraud Risk Management Policy


**Purpose:**
Define fraud risk ownership, detection standards, and response expectations.

## Scope
- Payments, cards, account takeover, and onboarding fraud.
- All customer channels and internal processes.

## Policy Statement
- Fraud detection must be real time for high-risk transactions.
- Alerts are triaged within defined service levels.
- Confirmed fraud is reported and remediated promptly.

## Roles and Responsibilities
- Fraud Operations: Investigate and resolve cases.
- Security: Provides threat intelligence and monitoring.
- Business Units: Enforce customer verification controls.

## Compliance and Exceptions
Exceptions require approval from the fraud risk owner.
