**Purpose:**
Define minimum security controls for systems, data, and access across the bank.

## Scope
- All information systems, networks, and applications.
- All employees, contractors, and vendors.

## Policy Statement
- Security controls follow defense-in-depth principles.
- Access is granted on least-privilege and reviewed regularly.
- Security incidents are reported and managed promptly.

## Roles and Responsibilities
- Security: Defines control standards and monitoring.
- Technology: Implements technical safeguards.
- Business Units: Ensure compliance in processes.

## Compliance and Exceptions
Exceptions require risk assessment and CISO approval.
