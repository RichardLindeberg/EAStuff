# Customer Trust and Data Privacy Capability

Strategic capability to protect customer data, maintain privacy, and communicate transparently to build and maintain customer trust.

## Description

This capability ensures the bank operates with the highest standards of data protection and privacy, transparently communicates with customers, and builds trust as a responsible financial institution.

## Capability Components

### People
- Data Protection Officer (DPO)
- Privacy specialists
- Security officers
- Customer communications specialists
- Compliance officers
- Training and awareness specialists

### Process
- Privacy impact assessment (PIA)
- Data handling procedures
- Consent management
- Breach response procedures
- Customer communication protocols
- Privacy training and awareness
- Regular privacy audits

### Technology
- Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA) tools
- Consent management platform
- Data encryption and tokenization
- Access control systems
- Audit logging and monitoring
- Customer communication platform
- Privacy notice management

### Information
- Privacy policies and notices
- Consent records
- Data retention schedules
- Data handling procedures
- Privacy assessment results
- Breach notification logs

## Current State

- Basic privacy compliance
- Manual consent management
- Fragmented communication
- Maturity: 2 - Repeatable

## Target State

- Privacy-by-design architecture
- Automated consent management
- Transparent, personalized communications
- Zero material breaches
- Customer trust index >70%
- Maturity: 4 - Managed

## Strategic Value

- Regulatory compliance (GDPR)
- Customer confidence and loyalty
- Competitive differentiation
- Reduced legal and regulatory risk
- Brand reputation protection
