# Customer Support Team

Multi-disciplinary team collaboration providing customer support services across all Nordic markets and channels.

## Description

The Customer Support Team represents the organized collaboration of customer service representatives, support agents, technical specialists, and supervisors working together to deliver exceptional customer support. Operating across multiple Nordic countries, this team handles customer inquiries, resolves issues, and ensures customer satisfaction through coordinated efforts.

## Team Structure

### First-Line Support
- **General Support Agents**: Handle routine inquiries, account questions, basic troubleshooting
- **Coverage**: 24/7 for critical issues, extended hours for general support
- **Languages**: All Nordic languages plus English

### Specialized Support
- **Technical Support Specialists**: Complex digital banking and technical issues
- **Product Specialists**: In-depth product knowledge and guidance
- **Fraud Prevention Team**: Security incidents and fraud investigation
- **Corporate Support**: Dedicated team for business banking customers

### Supervisory Layer
- **Team Leads**: Day-to-day management and escalation handling
- **Quality Assurance**: Call monitoring and service improvement
- **Training Coordinators**: Ongoing staff development

## Collaboration Model

### Cross-Functional Coordination
- Daily coordination with IT Operations for system issues
- Collaboration with Product teams for customer feedback
- Partnership with Compliance for regulatory matters
- Integration with Sales for customer retention

### Knowledge Sharing
- Shared knowledge base and documentation
- Daily briefings on system changes and issues
- Regular team meetings (Nordic-wide and local)
- Internal communication channels (Teams, SharePoint)

### Escalation Procedures
- Tier 1: General support agents (80% of cases)
- Tier 2: Specialized support (15% of cases)
- Tier 3: Expert resources and management (5% of cases)
- Critical escalation path to operations and management

## Technology & Tools

### Support Platforms
- CRM system (customer history and case management)
- Ticketing system for issue tracking
- Live chat and messaging platforms
- Video conferencing for video banking
- Screen sharing for technical support

### Knowledge Resources
- Searchable knowledge base
- Product documentation library
- Process guides and scripts
- Regulatory compliance reference
- Real-time system status dashboard

## Performance Metrics

### Service Level Indicators
- **First Contact Resolution**: Target 75%
- **Average Response Time**: <15 minutes (critical), <2 hours (general)
- **Customer Satisfaction**: Target >4.5/5.0
- **Case Resolution Time**: Target 24 hours (80% of cases)

### Quality Metrics
- Call quality monitoring scores
- Customer feedback ratings
- Compliance adherence rates
- Knowledge base utilization

## Nordic Market Specifics

### Multi-Market Coverage
- **Sweden**: Largest team, Stockholm-based with regional presence
- **Norway**: Oslo-based team with Bergen satellite
- **Denmark**: Copenhagen operations
- **Finland**: Helsinki-based bilingual team (Finnish/Swedish)

### Cultural Considerations
- Understanding of Nordic banking norms and expectations
- Familiarity with local payment systems (Swish, Vipps, MobilePay)
- Knowledge of country-specific regulations
- Sensitivity to language and cultural nuances

### Regulatory Requirements
- Data protection training (GDPR)
- AML/CTF awareness
- Consumer protection standards
- Complaint handling procedures

## Continuous Improvement

- Monthly service reviews and improvement initiatives
- Customer feedback analysis and action plans
- Technology updates and tool optimization
- Regular training and skill development
- Best practice sharing across markets
