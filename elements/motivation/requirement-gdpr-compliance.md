# GDPR Data Protection Compliance

All customer personal data must be processed in compliance with GDPR requirements including data minimization, consent management, and individual rights fulfillment.

## Scope

- Customer personal data handling
- Employee data management
- Vendor and partner data processing
- Cross-border data transfers
- Data retention and deletion

## Key Requirements

- Explicit consent for data collection and processing
- Data minimization (collect only necessary data)
- Individual rights (access, rectification, deletion, portability)
- Data breach notification within 72 hours
- Data Protection Impact Assessments (DPIA) for high-risk processing
- Privacy by design in systems and processes

## Compliance Measures

- Data Protection Officer (DPO) oversight
- Privacy policies and transparent communications
- Consent management systems
- Data lifecycle management
- Regular security audits and assessments
- Staff training and awareness programs
