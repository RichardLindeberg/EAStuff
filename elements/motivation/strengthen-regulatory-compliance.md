# Regulatory Compliance Excellence

Ensure full compliance with EU and Nordic regulatory frameworks including GDPR, MiFID II, and local banking regulations.

## Description

This goal represents the bank's commitment to maintaining the highest standards of regulatory compliance across all operations. Given the stringent regulatory environment in Northern Europe, compliance is a strategic imperative that protects the organization, customers, and stakeholders.

## Objectives

1. **Achieve GDPR Compliance**: Implement robust data protection and privacy controls
2. **Meet MiFID II Requirements**: Ensure investment services transparency and client reporting
3. **Comply with Basel III**: Maintain adequate capital ratios and risk management standards
4. **Satisfy PSD2 Regulations**: Enable secure open banking and payment services
5. **Adhere to National Requirements**: Meet specific requirements from Swedish, Norwegian, and Danish financial authorities

## Success Metrics

- Zero regulatory violations or fines
- 100% compliance with audit findings
- Customer data protection: Zero breaches
- Regulatory assessment rating: Excellent
- Time to remediate audit findings: <30 days

## Strategic Alignment

This foundational goal ensures the bank can operate legally and maintain customer trust. It supports overall business resilience and reputation management in highly regulated Nordic markets.
