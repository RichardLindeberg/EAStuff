# Process Automation Requirements

Critical operational processes must be automated to achieve efficiency targets, reduce manual work, and improve consistency.

## Target Processes

- Account opening and KYC/AML verification
- Loan application processing
- Customer onboarding workflow
- Payment processing
- Account reconciliation
- Document management and archival
- Routine customer service inquiries
- Transaction monitoring and fraud detection

## Automation Standards

- Manual effort reduction: >60% for target processes
- Processing time reduction: >50%
- Error rate reduction: >80%
- Scalability: Support 2x volume growth without additional staff
- Exception handling: Clear escalation to human review
- Audit trail: Complete logging and compliance documentation

## Implementation Approach

1. Identify and prioritize high-volume, low-complexity processes
2. Process mapping and optimization
3. RPA and workflow automation tools deployment
4. AI/ML for intelligent decision-making
5. Change management and staff retraining
6. Continuous improvement and optimization

## Success Metrics

- Automation coverage: >60% of operational processes
- Labor cost reduction: 20-25%
- Cycle time improvement: Average 50% reduction
- Quality improvement: 95%+ first-pass accuracy
- Employee satisfaction: Positive sentiment in redeployed roles
