# MiFID II Investment Services Compliance

Investment services must comply with MiFID II regulations ensuring investor protection, transparency, and fair market conduct.

## Scope

- Investment advisory services
- Securities trading and execution
- Portfolio management
- Suitability assessments
- Fee transparency

## Key Requirements

- Investor categorization (retail vs. professional)
- Suitability assessments before recommendations
- Clear disclosure of fees and costs
- Best execution obligations
- Conflict of interest management
- Regular reporting on investment performance
- Complex product warnings

## Compliance Measures

- Documented investment processes
- Advisor training and certification
- Compliance monitoring systems
- Transaction reporting to regulators
- Client reporting and statements
- Audit and compliance reviews

## Impact on Digital Services

- KYC/AML requirements integrated into onboarding
- Advisor availability for complex products
- Transparent fee disclosures in digital channels
- Appropriate investor categorization in mobile apps
