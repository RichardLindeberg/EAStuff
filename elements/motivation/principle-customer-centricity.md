# Customer-Centric Banking

The bank places customer needs, preferences, and experience at the center of all business decisions and operations.

## Principle Statement

In all decisions, processes, and service design, we prioritize understanding and serving customer needs. We measure success by customer outcomes and satisfaction, not just financial metrics.

## Implementation

- Regular customer research and feedback collection
- Customer journey mapping and optimization
- Service design that prioritizes ease and convenience
- Staff training focused on customer service excellence
- Performance metrics tied to customer satisfaction
- Transparent communication and honest advice
- Complaint resolution focused on customer restoration

## Measurement

- Net Promoter Score (NPS): >50
- Customer satisfaction index: >80%
- Complaint resolution rate: >95%
- Customer retention rate: >90%
- Recommendation rate: >60% would recommend

## Stakeholder Impact

- **Customers**: Better services, more convenient solutions
- **Employees**: Clear purpose and customer-focused guidelines
- **Shareholders**: Long-term customer loyalty and profitable growth
- **Society**: Trustworthy financial institution serving community needs
