# Sustainability and Social Responsibility

The bank is committed to environmental sustainability, social responsibility, and governance excellence as core business principles.

## Principle Statement

We operate with responsibility toward the environment, society, and stakeholders. We support sustainable development, promote social equity, and maintain transparent governance. Our business model considers long-term environmental and social impact.

## Environmental Commitment

- Carbon neutrality: Achieve net-zero emissions by 2030
- Sustainable financing: Promote renewable energy and green projects
- Digital-first services: Reduce paper and environmental footprint
- Sustainable supply chain: Responsible vendor practices
- Environmental governance: Board-level sustainability oversight

## Social Responsibility

- Financial inclusion: Accessible services for underserved populations
- Community support: Investment in Nordic communities
- Diversity and inclusion: Representative workforce and leadership
- Fair employment: Competitive compensation and safe workplace
- Customer protection: Fair practices and responsible lending

## Governance Excellence

- Board independence and diversity
- Executive compensation aligned with sustainability
- Stakeholder engagement and transparency
- Risk management and crisis preparedness
- Ethical business practices and anti-corruption

## Measurement

- Carbon emissions: -50% by 2028, net-zero by 2030
- ESG ratings: Top quartile in Nordic banking
- Renewable energy financing: >30% of new energy projects
- Diversity metrics: >40% women in leadership by 2027
- Community investment: >1% of net income

## Stakeholder Impact

- **Environment**: Reduced climate impact and environmental footprint
- **Society**: Positive social contribution and community support
- **Employees**: Purposeful work aligned with values
- **Customers**: Values-aligned banking partner
- **Investors**: ESG-focused investment opportunity
- **Regulators**: Proactive sustainability leadership
