# Strategic Ecosystem Partnerships

The bank must establish partnerships with fintech companies, service providers, and complementary financial services to create ecosystem value.

## Partnership Categories

- **Fintech Providers**: Robo-advisors, investment platforms, aggregators
- **Payment Service Providers**: Alternative payment methods, wallets
- **Insurance Partners**: Insurance products, underwriting services
- **Wealth Management**: Investment services, portfolio management
- **Real Estate**: Mortgage services, property technology
- **Energy/Utilities**: Sustainability-focused financing
- **Telecommunications**: Embedded finance in mobile services

## Partnership Standards

- Secure API integration
- Data sharing agreements and compliance
- Revenue sharing models
- Service level agreements (SLAs)
- Regular performance review and optimization
- Compliance and audit requirements
- Branding and customer experience guidelines

## Partner Onboarding Process

1. Partnership evaluation and business case
2. Security and compliance assessment
3. API integration and testing
4. Performance monitoring and optimization
5. Revenue accounting and settlement
6. Regular business reviews

## Risk Management

- Partner due diligence and assessment
- Service continuity and fallback procedures
- Data security and privacy assurance
- Regulatory compliance verification
- Reputational risk assessment
- Termination and offboarding procedures

## Success Metrics

- Number of active partnerships: >50 by 2027
- Partner transaction volume: >10% of digital transactions
- Partner-originated revenue: >5% of digital revenue
- Partner satisfaction rating: >4/5
- Time to partner integration: <90 days
